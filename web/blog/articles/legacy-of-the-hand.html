A line that remembers pressure

Hand carving leaves a different kind of precision. The mark is not irregular for its own sake; it contains pressure, hesitation, correction, and release. Those small decisions give the impression a human rhythm that a perfectly repeated cut cannot imitate.

Designed for one owner

A seal is personal because it is used as a signature. When an artisan carves for a specific name, the balance of each stroke changes with the owner in mind. The work becomes less like manufacturing a part and more like setting a name into a durable object.

That is why hand carving remains central to STONE SIGNATURE. It gives the stone a final presence that belongs to one person, one name, and one future history of use.
