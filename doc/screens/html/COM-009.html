Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 16, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how
Stone Signature (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and
protects your information when you use our app and services.

We collect information you provide directly, such as your
name, email address, and payment details when you create
an account or place an order. We may also collect information
automatically, including device information, app usage, and
analytics data to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide and maintain our
services, process your orders, personalize your experience,
communicate with you, and improve our app. We may also use
your information to send important updates and promotional
content, which you can opt out of at any time.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your
information with trusted service providers who assist us in
operating our app and delivering our services, subject to strict
confidentiality obligations. We may also share information
when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the
purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period
is required or permitted by law. You may request deletion of your
account and personal data at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy
or our data practices, please contact us at:

privacy@stonesignature.com
